Question #1...What is Bitcoin?

It’s crypto currency, meaning it’s currency that lives online. You can't feel it, you can't touch it, you can't hold it!

Yes
It’s a form of currency.

Yes
You can convert it to hard currency (called Fiat currency) like USD, AUD and any single currency in the world.

Yes
You can buy real things likes beers, products and even houses with it.

Yes
There are Bitcoin ATM’s popping up all around the globe.

What’s Bitcoin’s advantage?

It’s decentralised! It’s not tied to any one government, country or body of people meaning it’s left to its own devices. It's only worth what people are willing to pay for it.

It’s a political safe haven when a country or government landscape could be in uproar. Bitcoin’s value is driven from supply and demand!

What’s it’s disadvantages?

Because it is decentralised, it could all crash tomorrow with no notice and the dream will be over.

Which is why I'm only play with what you can afford to lose!

How am I doing it?!?

I’m buying packages through a mining company called USI Tech.

Basically, what happens is I buy a (1) package from USI-Tech worth the equivilant of €50 in Bitcoin. Each package lasts for approx. 140 business days.

What USI-Tech do is give me 140% return on my capital for that one package and they return approx. 1% to me per day in bitcoin.

I say "approx. 140 business days" becuase it's until 140% return is reached. One day can return anywhere from 0.75% to 1.25% therefor it could be more or less than 140 business days. So, if you work on approx. 1% per business day = 140 business days.

That 1% per day, I'm free to either let it compound and build up over time and withdraw as I like or I can use it to rebuy another package when my balance reached the equivilate of €50 in bitcoin.

While they return 140% worth of my initial captial, here is the even better news, it doesn’t take into account if the value of bitcoin goes up since my first puchase because they pay in bitcoin. If Bitcoin goes up, my return is greater than 140%.
